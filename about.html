About Me

Hello friends, let me introduce myself. My name is Lucky Surya Saputra. Here, I'd like to tell you about myself. Since childhood, I've always had a strong curiosity for new things. I believe that every challenge is an opportunity to learn, so I'm accustomed to working enthusiastically and consistently in completing tasks.

I'm disciplined, hardworking, and enjoy trying new things. When faced with a problem, I always strive to find creative solutions and never give up easily. In my work, I also prioritize teamwork, as I believe success is determined not only by individual ability but also by teamwork and good communication.

Outside of studying and school, I try to fill my time with various challenging and exciting activities. One of them is exploring nature through mountain climbing or with others. From there, I learn the meaning of togetherness, environmental stewardship, and how to face adversity calmly. Furthermore, I also enjoy riding motorcycles, which I consider not just entertainment or a hobby, but also a way to practice focus, try new things, balance, and courage when facing winding roads.

I believe that life should be lived with curiosity and resilience, as every challenge and joy brings valuable lessons that shape who I am. By combining my love of nature, adventure, and creativity, I not only discover new experiences but also strengthen my character, patience, and adaptability. This journey, whether on a winding road or through the pages of a book, reminds me that growth comes from stepping out of my comfort zone, appreciating the little things, and staying true to my passion.
